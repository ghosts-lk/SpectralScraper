- LinkedIn (most comprehensive)
- Crunchbase (funding data)
- Company Websites
- Email Databases

### **Step 2: Select Filters**

**Quick Method** ⚡:
1. Click a Quick Search Preset (e.g., "Tech VPs & CTOs")
2. All criteria auto-populate
3. Click "🚀 Launch Scraper"

**Custom Method** 🎨:
1. Manually check boxes in each category
2. Combine across multiple categories (filters are AND logic)
3. Watch **Active Criteria** section update in real-time
4. Remove individual criteria by clicking the × on tags

### **Step 3: Remove Criteria**

Three ways to manage selections:

1. **Individual removal**: Click × on any criteria badge
2. **Category management**: Uncheck individual boxes
3. **Full reset**: Click "Clear All" button

### **Step 4: Launch Scraper**

1. Confirm your sources are selected
2. Verify criteria in summary
3. Click "🚀 Launch Scraper"
4. Monitor progress in Operations tab

## 📊 Best Practices

### ✅ DO:

- **Combine 2-4 filters** for best results
- **Use presets** as starting templates
- **Start broad, then narrow** filters progressively
- **Check criteria summary** before launching
- **Export results** for follow-up tracking
- **Update filters** based on response rates

### ❌ DON'T:

- **Over-filter**: Selecting too many criteria reduces results
- **Mix unrelated criteria**: "Founders" + "Fortune 500" yields nothing
- **Ignore small datasets**: Very narrow filters may have <50 results
- **Forget data quality**: Verify imported leads manually
- **Leave empty filters**: Select at least one source

### **Q: I selected filters but got no results**

**A: This can happen with**:
- Overly specific combinations (3+ rare criteria)
- Rare industry + small company size
- Geographic region with limited data

**Solution**: Remove one filter and try again
